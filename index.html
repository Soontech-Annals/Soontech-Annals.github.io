General Description
Have you ever wanted to learn more about encoded storage technology in Minecraft?
We at Soontech are dedicated to documenting and sharing our knowledge, so that you can learn more about this
fascinating subject. Our goal is to make it easy for anyone to get started with encoded storage, regardless of their
experience level.
What is Encoded Storage?
An encoded storage is a system that uses redstone signals to sort and store items in Minecraft. Many fancy things can
be done with encoded storage, such as:
• Extremely fast sorting using splitters and encoders
• Remote call systems that allow you to access your items from anywhere in the world
• Autocrafting systems that restock your supplies automatically
• And much more!
